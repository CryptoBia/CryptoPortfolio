FAQ

Is it safe to enter my portfolio on this site?

As long as you don't share your personal portfolio URL your data should be safe. All portfolios are saved to the database in a completely anonymous fashion. This web app does not collect any cookies (except Third-Party Cookies from Disqus), does not have ads and is 100% open source. The source code can be viewed and changed on Github (My Cryptoportfolio).
